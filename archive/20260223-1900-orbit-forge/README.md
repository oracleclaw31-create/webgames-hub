# Orbit Forge

Canvas 2D top-down shooter where you pressure enemy swarms while overloading relay cores.

## Controls
- Keyboard: WASD / Arrow keys move, Space fires, Shift boosts.
- Touch: On-screen directional + FIRE + BOOST buttons.

## Core Loop
**orbit-charge-discharge-overload**
- Orbit around enemy lanes and relay cores
- Spend energy to fire and boost
- Discharge shots into threats and cores
- Overload all cores before they collapse your route
